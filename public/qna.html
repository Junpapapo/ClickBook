Frequently Asked Questions & Q&A

We've collected solutions and answers to common questions while using ClickBook Premium.

Q When I open a new tab, Chrome prompts: "Change back to Google?" Can this be disabled or controlled?

No, this popup cannot be programmatically controlled, disabled, or bypassed by extension developers.

This alert is an inherent, hardcoded security mechanism of the Chrome browser designed to protect users from malicious software (browser hijackers) that might modify search engines or new tab pages without permission.

To use ClickBook as your customized new tab page, you must manually click the [Keep changes] button the first time the prompt appears after installation.

Q I accidentally clicked "Restore settings" (or "Change back") and now my new tab or dashboard is a blank white screen, or won't open.

If you accidentally clicked [Restore settings], Chrome unilaterally revokes ClickBook's permission to override the new tab page. When this happens, the sync between ClickBook's internal state and the browser's blocked state becomes inconsistent, resulting in blank screens or unresponsive tabs.

You can easily resolve this by resetting the browser's extension permission using the following steps:

Fastest & Most Reliable Recovery Procedure:
  1. Type chrome://extensions/ in your Chrome address bar to open the Extension Management page.
  2. Find the ClickBook card and toggle the blue active switch OFF, then toggle it back ON.
  3. Open a blank new tab. Chrome will prompt you with the security confirmation again.
  4. This time, make sure to click the [Keep changes] button. Everything will immediately return to normal!
Q When importing bookmarks or registering large batches, it freezes or stays at "Registering..." for a long time.

This was a freezing/bottleneck issue that occurred when the browser's built-in local AI (Gemini Nano) environment was not fully configured or downloaded on the user's computer. This has been completely resolved in the v1.2.5 update.

ClickBook now pre-diagnoses the availability of the built-in local AI in real-time (under 1ms). If the AI is not ready or unavailable, it completely bypasses the local AI session creation and automatically falls back to our ultra-fast pattern-matching category classifier, ensuring a smooth and rapid import without freezing.

AI summaries and tags for the imported bookmarks will be processed asynchronously in a background thread, meaning there will be absolutely no interruption to your dashboard navigation or browser performance.

Q When Smart Tab Suspender triggers, will it suspend important tabs that shouldn't go to sleep? Premium

Don't worry! ClickBook's smart tab suspension algorithm is designed with user workflow safety in mind. The following tabs are fully excluded and protected from being forced into suspension (sleep):

  • Audio Playback Tabs: Background tabs playing music or videos (e.g., YouTube, music streaming sites).
  • Pinned Tabs: Tabs pinned to the left side of the tab bar.
  • Currently Active Tab: The tab you are currently viewing and working on.
  • Form Input Pages: Pages with active text inputs or forms to prevent data loss.
Q What data does the Secure Folder's automatic session shredder delete? Premium

When you designate confidential directories or sensitive domains as Secure Folders, a green `SEC` badge lights up on the Chrome toolbar icon while viewing those pages. The moment you close all related tabs and end your browsing session, the following tracking items collected by that domain (Origin) are permanently and automatically shredded:

  • Cookies: Auto-login sessions and tracking cookie data.
  • Local and Session Storage: LocalStorage and SessionStorage remnants.
  • Web Databases: IndexedDB and WebSQL data.
  • Cache & Service Workers: Cached files and background service workers.
  • Browsing History & File Residues: Individual history records and temporary files.

You never have to manually clear your Chrome browsing data; simply closing the secure tab takes care of your privacy perfectly.

Q How do I synchronize Chrome's native Tab Groups with ClickBook folders? Premium

ClickBook offers seamless two-way integration and preservation with Chrome's native tab groups:

  • Save Tab Group as ClickBook Folder: Click the ClickBook extension icon (popup) in your toolbar. It will list your currently open Chrome tab groups along with their color labels. Click the desired group and select [Save Group] to instantly create a ClickBook folder containing all those tabs.
  • Restore ClickBook Folder as Tab Group: Hover over any folder in your dashboard sidebar and click the Layer icon, or click [Open as Chrome Tab Group] in the folder detail view. Your ClickBook folder will instantly be restored as a native Chrome tab group, complete with its original name and color.
Q How can I back up my data or migrate bookmarks to a new computer?

ClickBook stores all bookmark data strictly inside your local computer to guarantee maximum privacy. Therefore, we provide a manual backup system.

Click the gear icon (Settings) in the top-right of your dashboard, then go to the Data Management tab. Click [Export Backup (JSON)] to download a file containing your entire folder structure, bookmark details, suspender configurations, and secure folders.

On your new computer or browser, simply navigate to the same settings page and upload your backup file using the [Import Backup] button. All folders, bookmarks, and even folder emojis will be fully restored in less than a second!

Q How do I use the Smart Clipper and Full-Text Search (FTS), and what are their features? Premium

The Smart Clipper runs in the background when saving a bookmark, automatically removing webpage clutter (such as ads, footers, navigation) to extract and store pure, high-quality body text locally in under 10ms.

The extracted text is immediately indexable and searchable via the Full-Text Search (FTS) engine:

  • Space-separated Multi-keyword Search: Type multiple terms separated by spaces in the search bar (e.g., React State Management) to find matches across titles, tags, summaries, and full webpage body text in real-time.
  • Zero Overhead: Built without heavy search libraries using native local indexing, providing lightning-fast matching across thousands of pages in less than 10ms.
Q What is Offline Reader and Zen Reader? Premium

The Offline Reader is an immersive reading overlay that reformats bookmark body texts into a clean, beautiful, book-like layout completely free of ads or sidebars.

  • Themes & Typography: Relax your eyes with customizable Light, Dark, or Sepia themes. Includes premium font selections optimized for English, Japanese, and Korean (Serif, Sans, Mono), and 4 font size options.
  • Automatic Table of Contents (TOC): Parses headings in the text to dynamically build a left-side navigation panel with smooth scroll-to-heading interactions.
  • Progress Tracker & Copy Tools: Visualize your reading progress with a scroll indicator bar and easily copy articles as raw Markdown or plain unformatted text.
  • Zen Reader Mode: Long personal memos written in the Memo Board also support this premium viewer, letting you read your ideas in a distraction-free environment.
