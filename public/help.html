Welcome to ClickBook

ClickBook is an innovative bookmark manager that uses Chrome's built-in AI (Gemini Nano) to automatically categorize and organize your links. Save any page with one click and find it later without the hassle of manual sorting.

Pro Tip: Pin the ClickBook icon to your toolbar for instant access and 1-click saving.

Getting Started

1-Click Save
Click the extension icon while browsing. AI analyzes the page and places it in the best folder automatically.
Keyboard Shortcut
Press Alt + S to save your active tab instantly without even moving your mouse.
Management Dashboard
Access all your bookmarks in a dedicated tab. Search, edit, move, and organize everything in one place.

AI Intelligent Features Core

Auto Categorization
Analyzes site titles and URLs to sort them into 8 broad categories like Technology, Design, and Business.
AI Bulk Reorganize
Tidies up your entire collection at once. It even backs up your current state automatically before running.
Semantic Search Beta
Search by describing what you need. Try "I'm looking for React tutorials" or "Latest news sites."

Folders & Management

  • Infinite Hierarchy: Create subfolders within folders to keep things organized.
  • Drag & Drop: Move sites and folders intuitively. Reorder top-level folders by dragging them between others.
  • Folder Locking: Prevent AI from moving bookmarks out of specific folders by using the lock icon.
  • Emoji Icons: Personalize your folders with any emoji to make them stand out.

Privacy & Security

100% Local Processing
ClickBook never connects to external servers. Your data stays ONLY on your computer (Local Storage). No one else can access it.
