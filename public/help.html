Intro & Installation

ClickBook is an innovative tool that uses Chrome's built-in AI (Gemini Nano) to intelligently categorize and manage your bookmarks. Save websites with a single click and find them easily later without the hassle of manual organization.

💡 Tip: Pin the extension icon to your toolbar for instant access anytime, anywhere.

Getting Started

1-Click Quick Save
Just click the ClickBook icon on any page you want to save. The AI analyzes the content and places it in the most appropriate folder.
Keyboard Shortcuts
No need to move your mouse. Press Alt + S to instantly save the current tab.
Management Dashboard
View all your saved bookmarks at a glance on the dedicated dashboard. Search, edit, move, and more.

AI Intelligent Features Core

AI Auto-Categorization
Automatically classifies bookmarks into various categories like Tech, Design, and Business by analyzing the page title and content.
AI Auto-Cleanup
Want to reorganize all your existing bookmarks at once? Click "AI Auto Cleanup" in the sidebar.
AI Semantic Search Beta
Goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand your intent. Search with sentences like "that cosmetics site I saw recently."
AI Highlight Clipper Premium
Drag any text on a webpage and save it via the right-click menu. The on-device AI analyzes the language (English/Japanese/Korean, etc.) and automatically generates a clean summarized note according to custom instruction rules.
Smart Clipper & Full-Text Search Premium
Upon saving a bookmark, the body text is cleaned (removing ads, navigation, and script noise) and saved locally in under 10ms. Type multiple terms separated by spaces in the search bar to find matching keywords anywhere inside the webpage content.

Productivity Tools

Memo Board
Add important notes to each bookmark and see them all together on the Memo Board.
Todo Board
Create todo lists linked to your bookmarks to systematically manage your reading or tasks.
Tag Board
Assign free tags regardless of the folder structure and filter bookmarks by your interests.
Bookmark Map
Understand the relationships between your bookmarks at a glance in a visual map format.
Smart Tab Suspender Premium
Puts inactive background tabs to sleep to reduce Chrome's RAM usage by up to 90%. Smartly filters audio-playing or pinned tabs, and restores them instantly by simply hovering your mouse.
Offline Reader & Zen Mode Premium
Provides an immersive, clutter-free reading overlay by removing ads and bloated layouts. Features custom Light/Sepia/Dark themes, serif/sans-serif font choices, automatic Table of Contents (TOC) navigation, and scroll progress. Memos also feature an elegant Zen Reader view.

Folder & Bookmark Management

  • Infinite Hierarchy: Create unlimited subfolders within folders for systematic organization.
  • Drag & Drop: Easily drag and drop sites or folders to where you want them.
  • Folder Lock: Lock specific folders to prevent their content from being changed during AI Auto Cleanup.
  • Custom Icons: Decorate your folders with unique emojis or icons to make them stand out visually.
  • Two-Way Tab Group Sync Premium: Backup Chrome's native tab groups into ClickBook folders all at once, and conversely restore folders instantly back into native Chrome tab groups while preserving their unique colors and titles.

Chrome Bookmark Sync

Import Bookmarks
Instantly import your existing Chrome bookmarks into ClickBook. You can even auto-categorize them with AI during the process.
Export to Chrome
Export your organized folder structure back to the native Chrome bookmark manager to sync with other devices.

Settings & Data Management

  • Theme Settings: Choose freely between Light, Dark, or System themes.
  • Backup & Restore: Export all your data to a JSON file for safe keeping and restore it anytime.
  • Data Reset: Delete all local data and start fresh. (Warning: Deleted data cannot be recovered.)

Privacy & Security

100% Local Operation
ClickBook does not communicate with any external servers. Your data is stored only inside your computer (Local Storage).
Secure Folder & Auto Session Sweeper Premium
Designate folders containing confidential information or financial pages as 'Secure Folders'.
  • Real-time Detection Badge: When you enter a secure site, a green `SEC` badge dynamically lights up on the Chrome toolbar icon to indicate active protection.
  • Entry Notification Toast: Upon page load completion, an elegant secure session notification toast appears in the bottom right corner once to provide peace of mind.
  • Auto Shred on Close: Once you close the tab after browsing, associated cookies, local storage, cache, IndexedDB, and browsing history are completely and automatically wiped for absolute privacy with no traces left behind.
